BTC2Fiat.me

B2F for short is a lightweight and distraction-free conversion tool, created for Bitcoin enthusiasts who value privacy and digital minimalism.

B2F is free from charts, click-bait news, FUD, candles, or any other irrelevant information. We don't collect any cookies to track you, nor do we sell your information or try to profit from it.

Open Source

The open source code is extremely lightweight (less than 30 KB). You're more than welcome to audit the Github repository and submit bugs.

Even better: become sovereign and launch it on your server or web host simply by uploading the source files:
